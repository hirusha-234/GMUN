GMUN'25 will take place on March 15-17, 2025 at the Royal College Colombo. The conference will begin with an opening ceremony at 9:00 AM on March 15th and conclude with the closing ceremony on March 17th at 5:00 PM.

Registration is open through our online portal. Schools should register through the School Registration option, while individual delegates can use the Private Delegate Registration. Complete the form, pay the registration fee, and you'll receive confirmation within 48 hours with your country and committee assignments.

The registration fee includes conference materials, access to all committee sessions, social events, refreshments throughout the conference, certificate of participation, and conference merchandise including a delegate kit.

Accommodation is not included in the standard registration fee. However, we have partnered with several hotels near the venue that offer special rates for GMUN delegates. International delegates can request accommodation assistance during registration for additional information.
